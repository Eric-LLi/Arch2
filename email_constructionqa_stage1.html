The Report will have noted a number of items that require attention – then assisting you with completion on site.

  • Unrestricted access to all parts of a property is in practice seldom available at the time of assessment but the report is prepared with care and diligence within the limits of available access. Please note that a charge will be made for a re-assessment depending on the time taken.
